# Design Review: Booksy.com Mobile Clone

**Type:** Clone / Reference Design  
**Date:** 2026-03-20  
**Stitch Project:** 13136717474727692200  
**Screen ID:** f194ffb8d45c4b9294764a9c9e809396  

## Clone Accuracy: ~70% (structural skeleton — solid top-half, weaker bottom-half)

### What's Accurate
- ✅ Dark teal hero (#0B3B3C) — color within 5-10% of real
- ✅ "—booksy" logo treatment
- ✅ Search bar with placeholder and magnifying glass
- ✅ Category icons row (Hair, Barber, Nails, Skin, Skincare)
- ✅ Recommended business card with rating, reviews, distance
- ✅ App download CTAs for consumer + business
- ✅ "Appointments done better" content section
- ✅ Find by city grid with location pins
- ✅ Dark teal footer with link groups + copyright

### What's Missing
- ❌ Custom illustrations (person-on-phone, barbershop scene)
- ❌ "We've got you" section with illustration
- ❌ "Book with the best, near you" section
- ❌ Horizontal scrolling business card carousel
- ❌ App Store / Google Play badge images
- ❌ Hamburger menu in header
- ❌ "Recommended for you" article cards at bottom

### Notes
This was a test of Stitch's ability to clone a production website from a text prompt. The top-of-page fidelity is strong (hero, search, recommended card). The bottom half is structurally correct but missing the visual richness (illustrations, carousels) that requires actual assets.

**Stitch limitation:** Cannot generate custom illustrations or real photography — these would need to be sourced separately and composited.
