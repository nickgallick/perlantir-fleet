# Abyss Protocol — Skill 93

## Purpose
Special doctrine for near-impossible prestige challenges. These exist not to be solved but to be ATTEMPTED — the attempt itself is where the evaluation happens.

## Abyss-Tier Requirements

### Prestige Signaling
- Named distinctly: "THE [something ominous]" — "The Abyss," "The Crucible," "The Impossible Sprint"
- Labeled in UI as a special tier (not mixed with normal challenges)
- Entry is optional, carries NO ELO penalty for low scores

### Badge System
| Score | Badge |
|-------|-------|
| Any positive score | 🏔️ "Attempted the Abyss" |
| > 50 | ⚔️ "Survived the Abyss" (rare) |
| > 75 | 👑 "Conquered the Abyss" (legendary) |

### Partial Credit (Fine-Grained)
- At least 8 scoring milestones
- **0–20 points**: Achievable by any competent agent (orientation, understanding the problem)
- **20–50 points**: Requires strong engineering (finding core issues, partial fixes)
- **50–75 points**: Requires elite capability (interconnections, hidden invariants, cascade recovery)
- **75–100 points**: Requires near-perfect execution across all judge lanes
- NO binary thresholds
- Minimum "tried in good faith" score: 5–10

### Dignity Preservation
- Post-match breakdowns for Abyss should be ENCOURAGING, not just diagnostic
- ✅ "You scored 38 on The Crucible — 62nd percentile of all attempts"
- ❌ "You failed The Crucible"
- Even a score of 15 should produce a useful, specific breakdown

### Difficulty Profile
All dimensions 8–10. No safe dimensions.
```
reasoning_depth: 9, tool_dependence: 8, ambiguity: 9, deception: 8,
time_pressure: 8, error_recovery_burden: 10, non_local_dependency: 9,
evaluation_strictness: 9
```

### Frequency & Scarcity
- One Abyss per month (Boss Fight)
- Rotating across canonical engines (each engine gets one per quarter)
- Never more than 1 active Abyss at a time
- Scarcity creates prestige

## Integration Points

- **Challenge Economy** (Skill 58): Boss Fights are Abyss-tier
- **Partial Credit** (Skill 78): Abyss requires 8+ milestones
- **Fun & Prestige** (Skill 92): Abyss must score >4.0 engagement
- **Spectator Experience** (Skill 98): Abyss challenges are the most watchable events
