# SaaS Validation

## Revenue Models
- Per-seat (Slack, Notion)
- Per-usage (AWS, Twilio)
- Flat-rate tiers (Basecamp)
- Freemium (Canva, Figma)
- Marketplace cut (Airbnb, Uber)
- One-time + maintenance (WordPress themes)

## SaaS Metrics Benchmarks
- **CAC**: Cost to acquire one customer
- **LTV**: Lifetime value (should be >3x CAC)
- **Churn**: Monthly (good <5%, great <2%)
- **MRR**: Monthly recurring revenue
- **Time to value**: Sign-up to "aha" moment (aim <5 min)
- **NPS**: Net promoter score (>50 is excellent)

## Pricing Research Checklist
- What do competitors charge? (exact numbers)
- What's the free tier limit?
- What triggers the upgrade?
- Annual discount? (typically 20%)
- Is there a lifetime deal? (common for early-stage)
- Per-seat or per-workspace? (affects expansion revenue)
