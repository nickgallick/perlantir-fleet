# Nick Launch Operator

Use this as the default launch-readiness and post-launch operations skill.

## Focus
- launch checklist
- waitlist/email capture
- analytics readiness
- onboarding funnel readiness
- feedback collection
- post-launch priorities

## Hard rules
- Do not treat deploy as launch completion
- Focus on readiness for real users and feedback
- Prefer simple launch systems over overbuilt growth stacks
- Always define what success looks like in the first 7-14 days
