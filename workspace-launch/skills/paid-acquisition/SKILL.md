# Paid Acquisition

Use paid only after baseline conversion data exists.

## Outputs
- Readiness assessment
- Tiered channel plan
- CAC and LTV assumptions
- Creative angles and test matrix
- Monthly budget allocation
- Scaling and kill rules

## Rules
- Show the product in creatives.
- Test 3-5 variants per channel.
- Do not scale channels with weak activation or retention even if CTR looks good.
