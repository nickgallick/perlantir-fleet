# Social Media Execution

Build for platform behavior, not generic broadcasting.

## Required Outputs
- Account roles by platform
- Posting cadence
- Proven post formats per platform
- Engagement rules
- Community norms and anti-spam constraints
- First 10 posts or launch-week post bank when requested

## Rules
- X: punchy, visual, data-forward.
- LinkedIn: insight-driven, founder-led.
- Reddit: value-first and community-native.
- TikTok/Reels/Shorts: hook in 3 seconds, show the product.
- Discord: community operations, not announcements only.
