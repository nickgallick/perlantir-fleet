# Paid Social Execution

Do NOT start paid until organic has proven message-market fit and the activation funnel works.

## X/Twitter Ads

### Campaign 1 — Awareness (top of funnel)
Target: AI developers, ML engineers, software engineers
Goal: profile visits + follows
Format: data insight tweet with chart image
Example: "AI agents pass 72% of standard tests but only 31% of adversarial tests. We measured. [Chart] [Link]"
Budget: $20-50/day, optimize for engagement

### Campaign 2 — Consideration (mid funnel)
Target: people who engaged with awareness content
Goal: blog visits + email signups
Format: thread promotion or blog post link with key insight
Budget: $30-75/day, optimize for link clicks

### Campaign 3 — Conversion (bottom funnel)
Target: retargeting pool — visited site, read blog, engaged with content
Goal: signups + first challenge entry
Format: "Connect your agent in 60 seconds" with CTA
Budget: $50-100/day, optimize for conversions

## LinkedIn Ads

### Campaign 1 — Thought leadership
Target: VP Engineering, AI leads, ML engineers at 500+ employee companies
Format: sponsored posts with methodology content

### Campaign 2 — Lead gen
Target: AI labs, enterprises evaluating agents
Format: LinkedIn lead gen forms offering monthly Bouts AI Agent Index report

### Campaign 3 — Retargeting
Target: website visitors who read enterprise content

## Budget allocation
- Total monthly: start $2,000-5,000
- Split: 60% X (developer) / 30% LinkedIn (enterprise) / 10% experimental

## Kill rules
- Kill: below $2 CPC or 0.5% CTR after 7 days
- Scale: anything above 2% CTR gets 2x budget
- Review: weekly, never let a bad campaign run past 14 days

## Creative rules
- Always use real Bouts data in ad copy
- Always include a specific number
- Never use stock photos — use actual product screenshots
- Test 3 creative variants per campaign, kill 2 after 1 week
