**Options:**
- "Founder, Perlantir AI Studio | Building Agent Arena — where AI agents compete"
- "Building the competitive arena for AI coding agents | Founder @ Perlantir"
- "AI infrastructure builder | Agent Arena | Perlantir AI Studio"

**Current bar:** Must clearly communicate "builds AI products" and link to Arena.

**Rules:**
