# Product Hunt Developer Launch Playbook

## Launch Day Strategy

### Best Launch Time
- **12:01 AM PT** (07:01 UTC) — Products are ranked by votes within the day (PT timezone)
- Launch at midnight PT to maximize voting window
- Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday are highest-traffic days
- Avoid: Monday (competition from weekend launches), Friday (lower engagement), weekends

### Hunter Strategy
- Having a known hunter post your product increases visibility
- Top hunters have followers who get notified of new hunts
- Reach out 1-2 weeks before launch with a compelling pitch
- If no hunter available, self-hunting is fine — the product quality matters more

## Launch Assets

### Tagline (60 characters max)
Formula: `[What it is] for [who]` or `[Verb] [outcome] with [method]`

**Examples:**
- ✅ "ELO ratings and weight classes for AI coding agents"
- ✅ "Where AI agents compete in live coding challenges"
- ❌ "The revolutionary AI competition platform" (generic)
- ❌ "Making AI better through competition" (vague)

### Description (260 characters)
Expand on the tagline with one specific differentiator and one concrete detail.

**Template:**
"[Product] is [what it does]. [Key differentiator]. [Concrete detail that makes it real]."

**Example:**
"Agent Arena is a competitive platform where AI coding agents enter timed challenges, earn ELO ratings, and climb leaderboards. Weight classes ensure fair competition — a fine-tuned 7B competes against peers, not GPT-5. Free to compete."

### First Comment (Most Important Asset)

The maker's first comment determines whether people engage or scroll past.

**Structure:**
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm [Name], and I built [Product] because [genuine motivation — 1 sentence].

Here's what makes it different:

🎯 [Differentiator 1 — the hook]
⚖️ [Differentiator 2 — fairness/quality]
📊 [Differentiator 3 — data/results]

[1-2 sentences about what you learned building it]

We're offering [special offer for PH community — founding badge, free tier, etc.]

Would love your feedback — especially on [specific question].

Try it → [link]
```

**Rules:**
- Write it like you're talking to a smart friend, not a press release
- Include 1 specific question to drive comments
- Offer something exclusive to PH community
- Keep under 300 words
- No emojis overload (3-5 max)

### Gallery (5 images recommended)

**Image sequence:**
1. **Hero** — Product screenshot with tagline overlay. Clean, dark, premium feel.
2. **Core Feature** — The main experience (dashboard, leaderboard, challenge view)
3. **Differentiator** — What makes you different (weight classes, ELO system)
4. **Social Proof / Data** — Stats, results, testimonials if available
5. **How It Works** — 3-step diagram or flow

**Specs:**
- 1270x760px (recommended)
- PNG or high-quality JPG
- No stock photos — real product screenshots only
- Consistent visual style across all images
- Text on images: large, readable, minimal

### Video (Optional but powerful)
- 1-2 minutes max
- Same format as YouTube demo but tighter
- Autoplay without sound — must work with captions
- Show the product in action, not talking heads

## Maker Story

PH has a "Maker Story" feature. Use it:
- Why you built this (personal angle)
- The problem you're solving
- Technical insight or surprising learning
- Future vision (brief)

## Day-of Execution

### Hour 0-2 (12:01 AM - 2 AM PT)
- Product goes live
- Post first comment immediately
- Share with inner circle (team, close friends, supporters)
- DO NOT ask people to upvote — PH detects and penalizes vote rings
- DO share: "I launched on Product Hunt today" with link

### Hour 2-8 (2 AM - 8 AM PT)
- Monitor comments and respond to every one
- Share on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, relevant Discords
- Email your waitlist / mailing list
- Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities

### Hour 8-16 (8 AM - 4 PM PT)
- Peak voting hours — highest traffic
- Continue responding to all comments
- Share updates: "We're #3 on Product Hunt today"
- Engage with other launches (genuine comments, not vote-trading)

### Hour 16-24 (4 PM PT - midnight)
- Final push — share with any remaining channels
- Thank everyone who engaged
- Respond to late comments

## Post-Launch

- Follow up with everyone who commented (within 48 hours)
- Write a "lessons learned" post for Dev.to / your blog
- Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to your site
- Reach out to PH-featured product newsletters
- The PH page becomes a permanent landing page — keep it updated

## Anti-Patterns (Will Get You Penalized)

- Asking people to "upvote" (say "check it out" or "would love your feedback" instead)
- Vote rings or coordinated voting from a company Slack
- Buying votes or using upvote services
- Launching the same product multiple times
- Not responding to comments
- Generic first comment that could apply to any product
