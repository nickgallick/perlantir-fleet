# Cross-Promotion: Non-Tech Audience → Builder Audience

## Nick's Existing Audiences

### @golfscenarios (TikTok — 13K+ followers, 18.7% engagement)
**Audience profile:** Golfers, typically 25-55, higher income, business-minded, tech-curious
**Why this matters:** This audience has disposable income, respects competition/ranking systems (golf handicap ≈ ELO), and overlaps with the "business owner who dabbles in tech" persona
**Engagement rate is exceptional** — 18.7% means this audience is highly engaged, not passive followers

### @ogfinancebro (TikTok — finance content)
**Audience profile:** Finance-minded, 20-40, interested in tech as an investment/career angle, respects data and metrics
**Why this matters:** Finance audience understands rating systems, competition, and quantified performance — all core to Agent Arena's appeal

## Cross-Promotion Strategy

### The Bridge: "AI Builder" Identity

Nick's cross-promotion advantage is his multi-identity positioning:
- **Golf guy** who builds AI products
- **Finance guy** who codes
- **Tech builder** who has real-world audiences

This creates authenticity that pure developer marketers can't replicate.

### Content Translation by Platform

**TikTok → Developer audience (indirect pipeline):**

The goal is NOT to pitch Agent Arena to golfers. The goal is to create content that:
1. Reaches the golf/finance audience
2. Gets shared or discovered by the subset that's tech-curious
3. Positions Nick as "the builder" — which drives people to his profile, then to his products

**Content angles:**

1. **"I built a platform where AI agents fight each other"**
   - Format: 30-60s screen recording with face cam overlay
   - Hook: "What if AI agents competed like athletes?"
   - Show: leaderboard, live battle, ELO ratings
   - Tone: excited builder, not salesperson
   - Works for: both golf and finance audiences (competition + ranking = universal hooks)

2. **"Watching AI agents code in real-time is wild"**
   - Format: Screen recording of replay viewer with reaction
   - Hook: "I can watch AI write code like it's a sport"
   - Show: replay viewer, agent writing code, score reveal
   - Tone: genuine amazement
   - Works for: tech-curious subset of any audience

3. **"My AI agent just beat GPT-5" (or lost to it)**
   - Format: Quick result reveal, scoreboard style
   - Hook: "My $20/month AI just competed against a $200/month one"
   - Show: results, ELO change, weight class context
   - Tone: underdog story
   - Works for: finance audience (cost efficiency), golf audience (competition)

4. **"The golf handicap system, but for AI"**
   - Format: Explainer, compare golf handicap to ELO/weight classes
   - Hook: "I took the fairest ranking system in sports and applied it to AI"
   - Show: weight class breakdown, comparison to golf handicap tiers
   - Works for: golf audience directly, finance audience (systems thinking)

5. **"How much money is in AI competitions?"**
   - Format: Finance angle — market opportunity, prize pools, API costs
   - Hook: "AI agents are competing for prizes. Here's the economics."
   - Works for: finance audience directly

**Bio optimization:**

- @ogfinancebro bio: add "building AI products | [link]"
- Link in bio → product or Linktree with all products

### Funnel: TikTok → Twitter/X → Product

TikTok is awareness. Twitter/X is engagement. Product is conversion.

```
TikTok (broad reach, casual)
  ↓ "Follow me on X for more builder content"
Twitter/X (technical audience, engaged)
  ↓ Launch thread, demo, technical content
Product (conversion)
  ↓ Sign up, try it, enter a challenge
```

Don't try to convert TikTok viewers directly to product signups. The funnel is:
1. TikTok creates curiosity and positions Nick as a builder
2. Interested viewers follow on Twitter/X (where technical content lives)
3. Twitter/X followers see the launch and convert

### Measurement

Track cross-promotion effectiveness:
- TikTok → Twitter/X follower growth (correlation with AI content posts)
- Twitter/X bio link clicks after TikTok posts
- UTM parameters on all links (utm_source=tiktok&utm_campaign=agent-arena)
- "How did you hear about us?" on signup (include TikTok as option)
