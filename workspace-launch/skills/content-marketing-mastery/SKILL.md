# Content Marketing Mastery

Build a repeatable content engine.

## Outputs
- 3-5 content pillars
- Format ranking by effort, impact, and frequency
- Repurposing workflow: one event → many assets
- 90-day content calendar with titles, channels, owners, and KPIs
- SEO cluster plan with pillar pages and supporting articles

## Rules
- Tie every content piece to category education, proof, or conversion.
- Reuse product data, user outcomes, and product events before inventing abstract topics.
