## SEO article formula
Every article follows this structure:

1. **Hook** — first paragraph answers the search query directly
2. **Bouts data** — unique data nobody else has (solve rates, score gaps, archetype frequencies)
3. **Methodology** — why our data is trustworthy (5-judge system, contamination resistance)
4. **Actionable next step** — sign up, enter a challenge, read the report

## Target cadence
2-3 SEO articles per week.
One per cluster, rotating quarterly.
