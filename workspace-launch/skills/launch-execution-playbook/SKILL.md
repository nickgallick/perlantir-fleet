# Launch Execution Playbook

Use this skill as the live runbook for Arena launch execution.

## Objective
Create the perception that Arena is suddenly everywhere among the right technical audiences, while keeping the product and analytics stable enough to capitalize on attention.

## Day -14 to Day -8
- finalize hero message
- finalize screenshots
- cut demo clip
- prepare Product Hunt assets
- prepare Show HN draft
- prepare 3 Reddit variants
- verify UTM links
- test signup flow end to end
- identify 20-50 warm contacts

## Day -7 to Day -3
- warm relevant creators and communities
- finalize launch thread and founder post
- record short-form clip
- prepare FAQ for comments
- confirm challenge/replay pages work on mobile

## Day -2 to Day -1
- pre-schedule X posts
- prep Product Hunt listing
- prep HN draft in text file
- prep Discord announcement
- prep email blast and non-opener resend
- confirm analytics dashboards are live

## Contingency plans
### If Product Hunt underperforms
- do not anchor the whole launch on PH rank
- shift focus to direct site traffic, HN, X, and clips
- use PH asset as proof, not primary engine

### If Reddit removes a post
- rewrite in discussion form
- reduce explicit CTA
- post from learning angle not launch angle

### If launch traffic is high but signup weak
- simplify hero
- make replay/watch path more prominent
- move proof higher on page

### If signup strong but first challenge weak
- add quick-start CTA in app
- trigger follow-up email faster
- reduce setup friction and provide one-command copy

## Owner checklist
- Nick: founder post, top replies, key warm outreach
- Launch: copy, timing, channel adaptation, comment monitoring
- Product/build: patch issues, watch auth/setup, monitor analytics

## Launch-day non-negotiables
- reply everywhere quickly
- log channel quality, not only volume
- capture quotes/screenshots
- ship learnings into follow-up content within 24h
