# Partnership and Ecosystem Strategy

Build partnerships that create distribution or product value, not vanity logos.

## Partnership types
- integration partners
- content partners
- sponsored challenges or campaigns
- community partners
- enterprise or bounty partners

## Outputs
- partner tier list
- offer structure
- outreach angles
- pilot collaboration plan
- ROI proof approach

## Rules
- Lead with value for the partner.
- Start with a specific small pilot before asking for a broader deal.
