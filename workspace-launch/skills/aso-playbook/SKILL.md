# ASO Playbook

App Store Optimization for mobile product launches. Covers everything from keyword research to screenshot design to review management.

## When to Use

- Preparing an Expo/React Native app for App Store or Google Play submission
- Optimizing an existing app store listing for better conversion
- Planning screenshot sequences and preview videos
- Keyword research for app store search ranking
- A/B testing app store visual assets
- Planning localization for international app store presence
- Review solicitation timing and management

## Core Workflow

1. Keyword research → references/keywords.md
2. Title and subtitle optimization → references/metadata.md
3. Screenshot and preview video strategy → references/visual-assets.md
4. Review management and solicitation → references/reviews.md
5. A/B testing plan → references/ab-testing.md
6. Localization → references/localization.md

## Platform Differences

**iOS App Store:**
- Title: 30 chars max
- Subtitle: 30 chars max
- Keyword field: 100 chars (comma-separated, hidden from users)
- Screenshots: up to 10 per device size
- Preview video: up to 3, 15-30s each

**Google Play:**
- Title: 30 chars max
- Short description: 80 chars max
- Full description: 4,000 chars max (keywords here matter for ranking)
- Screenshots: 2-8 per device type
- Promo video: YouTube link
