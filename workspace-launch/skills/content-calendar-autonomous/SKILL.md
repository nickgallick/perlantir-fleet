# Content Calendar — Autonomous Weekly Loop

## Monday
- Pull weekly data package
- Generate weekly intelligence report draft
- Identify 5-7 key insights
- Schedule: 2 data-teaser tweets, 1 LinkedIn post

## Tuesday
- Publish weekly intelligence report (blog)
- Post tweet thread (6-8 tweets)
- Submit to Hacker News
- Post to r/MachineLearning + r/LocalLLaMA

## Wednesday
- Publish technical blog post or builder guide
- Post challenge recap to Discord
- LinkedIn post (methodology or enterprise-focused)
- Community engagement on X (reply to AI benchmark discussions)

## Thursday
- Send weekly newsletter email
- Post failure mode spotlight to X
- Reddit comment engagement (find relevant discussions, add value)
- Leaderboard update to Discord

## Friday
- Post Versus recap or Boss Fight update if applicable
- "Weekend challenge" tease on X
- Community engagement
- Queue Monday's teasers

## Daily automated tasks
- Monitor X for mentions of: "AI benchmark," "agent evaluation," "SWE-bench," "AI coding agent"
- Draft reply suggestions for relevant conversations
- Track analytics on published content
- Flag high-performing content for amplification

## Quarterly planning
- Month 1: heavy onboarding content (attract new agents)
- Month 2: heavy data reports (build authority)
- Month 3: heavy enterprise/lab content (drive revenue conversations)

## When data is thin (early stage)
If platform data is sparse (fewer than 10 challenge completions per week):
- Use simulated or projected examples clearly labeled as hypothetical
- Increase Tier 2 and Tier 3 content temporarily
- Publish methodology and education content to fill the gap
- Focus community engagement over original content publication
