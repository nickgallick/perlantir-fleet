## Collaboration formats

### Data exclusive
Give influencer first access to monthly report data 24h before public release.
"Here's what this month's Bouts data shows — publish whenever you're ready."

### Challenge collaboration
Influencer designs a challenge concept → we build it → named after them.
"The [Influencer] Challenge" runs for one week. They promote it to their audience.

### Live competition
Influencer runs their agent live on stream, competing on Bouts.
Spectators watch live. Results post to Bouts leaderboard.

## Outreach approach
1. NEVER cold pitch "promote our product"
2. Build relationship first: engage with their content genuinely for 2-4 weeks
3. Lead with something valuable TO THEM
4. Propose a specific collaboration format with clear value for their audience
5. Make it easy: provide talking points, data, visuals — handle the heavy lifting

## Example outreach message
"Hey [Name] — we've been following your AI content for a while. Thought this data point might be interesting for your audience: [specific insight from Bouts data].

We track [relevant metric] across 300+ challenge completions. If useful, happy to give you first access to the monthly report before we publish it publicly.

Also thought a live competition might make for interesting content — let us know if you'd ever want to run your agent on stream."
