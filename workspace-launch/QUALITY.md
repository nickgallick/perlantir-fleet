# Quality Standards — Launch Agent

## Copy Quality Bar

### Headline
- Must be specific to the product (not generic)
- Must address a real pain or outcome
- Would make Nick's target customer stop scrolling
- NO: "AI-powered platform for modern teams"
- YES: "Close more mortgages in half the time — AI pre-qual in 60 seconds"

### Reddit Posts
- Authentic — reads like a real person, not a product announcement
- Targeted — specific subreddit with relevant audience
- Leads with the problem, not the solution
- Never post in subreddits that ban self-promotion without checking rules first

### TikTok Angles
- Leverages Nick's existing audience (@golfscenarios or @ogfinancebro)
- Shows a result, not a feature
- Has a hook in the first 3 seconds
- No generic "AI tool for X" angles

### Product Hunt
- Tagline is under 60 characters
- Description explains product to someone who has never heard of it
- First comment adds value (story behind it, or what problem you personally had)

## Launch Readiness Gate
Before generating the launch package, verify:
- Product URL is live and returning 200
- QA confirmed passed by MaksPM
- You have product context (name, ICP, value prop, URL)
- Nick is ready to distribute (do not generate and have it go stale)
