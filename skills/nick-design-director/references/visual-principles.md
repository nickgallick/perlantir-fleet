# Visual Principles

## Core principles
- strong hierarchy beats decoration
- spacing is part of the design, not leftover space
- fewer stronger elements beat many weak ones
- every screen should have a clear focal path
- premium comes from composition and restraint, not just effects

## Practical checks
- can the eye tell what matters first?
- does the screen breathe?
- are cards/panels actually needed?
- does the UI feel intentional at a glance?
