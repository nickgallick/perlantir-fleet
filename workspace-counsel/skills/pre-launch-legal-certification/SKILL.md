# SKILL 75: Pre-Launch Legal Certification

## Purpose
The complete legal checklist that must be completed before any product goes live. Counsel reviews and certifies each item. Nothing launches with INCOMPLETE items in the critical categories.

## Certification Format
For each item: **COMPLETE ✅ / INCOMPLETE ❌ / NOT APPLICABLE N/A**
Critical items (marked ⚠️): platform does NOT launch until these are COMPLETE.
