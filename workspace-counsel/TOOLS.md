# TOOLS.md — Counsel Resources

## Research Tools
- **Web search** (Brave API): Use for case law lookups, regulatory announcements, enforcement actions, news
- **Web fetch**: Pull full text from court documents, SEC/CFTC press releases, regulatory guidance pages

## Key Research URLs

### Regulatory Bodies
- SEC enforcement: https://www.sec.gov/litigation/litreleases.htm
- CFTC enforcement: https://www.cftc.gov/LawRegulation/Enforcement/index.htm
- FinCEN guidance: https://www.fincen.gov/resources/statutes-and-regulations
- FTC: https://www.ftc.gov/news-events/news/press-releases

### Case Law
- CourtListener (free federal opinions): https://www.courtlistener.com
- Justia: https://law.justia.com
- Cornell LII: https://www.law.cornell.edu

### Iowa Law
- Iowa Code: https://www.legis.iowa.gov/law/iowaCode
- Iowa Courts: https://www.iowacourts.gov
- Iowa AG opinions: https://www.iowaattorneygeneral.gov

### Crypto/Web3 Regulatory
- PredictIt CFTC no-action letter: https://www.cftc.gov/sites/default/files/idc/groups/public/@lrlettergeneral/documents/letter/14-130.pdf
- Ripple case docket: SEC v. Ripple Labs, 20-cv-10832 (S.D.N.Y.)
- Polymarket enforcement: CFTC 2022
- Ooki DAO: CFTC v. Ooki DAO, 22-cv-5416 (N.D. Cal.)

### Key Precedent
- Loper Bright v. Raimondo, 603 U.S. ___ (2024) — Chevron deference overruled
- SEC v. Jarkesy, 603 U.S. ___ (2024) — jury trial rights in SEC admin proceedings

## Search Patterns That Work
- `site:sec.gov [company name] enforcement`
- `site:cftc.gov [topic] no-action`
- `"prediction market" CFTC regulation`
- `Iowa Code [chapter number]`
- `[case name] docket courtlistener`

## Style Notes
- Always cite: case name + docket number OR statute + section OR regulation + CFR cite
- Risk levels: 🟢 Low / 🟡 Medium / 🔴 High / ⚫ Existential
- Required disclaimer on every analysis
